Welcome to Our KDT-website

We are delighted to welcome you to a place where tradition meets taste. Our mission is to share the authentic flavors of Kazakh cuisine and preserve the culinary heritage of our people. Here, you will discover timeless recipes, learn about the history behind each dish, and experience the warm spirit of hospitality that defines Kazakh culture.

  • Beshbarmak – the national dish of meat and noodles.
  • Baursaks – golden fried bread, perfect with tea.
  • Kumis – a refreshing drink made from fermented mare’s milk.

Beshbarmak

The national dish of Kazakhstan made with boiled meat and noodles.

Baursaks

Golden fried bread, a staple in Kazakh cuisine, usually served with tea.

Kumis

A refreshing fermented mare’s milk drink, rich in tradition.
